Made You Look (Official Music Video)

"Made You Look" is a song by Meghan Trainor from her album "Takin' It Back," released in 2022. The song is inspired by Trainor's experiences with body image issues after giving birth, particularly her C-section, and encourages listeners to embrace their natural beauty and confidence. It features a doo-wop sound and has become a viral sensation on TikTok, where it sparked a dance challenge. The song's lyrics reflect a message of self-love and empowerment, urging listeners to find their appeal in their unique qualities rather than external appearances.

Whoops (Live from The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon)

"Whoops" is a pop-doo-wop break-up song by Meghan Trainor from her 2024 album Timeless. It is a sassy, high-energy anthem about an ex-partner who made a mistake and the new, less-impressive person he is now with. The song features confident lyrics about moving on and, in the video, features scenes of smashing a set with a baseball bat.

Meghan Trainor's World Premiere of 'No Excuses'

"No Excuses" is a 2018 dance-pop song by Meghan Trainor, serving as the lead single from her album Treat Myself. It is an empowering, uptempo anthem about demanding respect, often described as a modern "R-E-S-P-E-C-T". The track features a confident, "bouncy" sound with lyrics that call out disrespectful behavior and promote assertiveness.
